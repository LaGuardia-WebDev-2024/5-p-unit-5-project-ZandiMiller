What Is The Good Place?

The series follows Eleanor Shellstrop, a dead woman who is placed in a Heaven-like utopian afterlife designed and supervised by Michael. However, Eleanor knows she did not deserve to get into the Good Place, as she lived a dishonorable life. To avoid being found out and sent to the Bad Place, Eleanor attempts to hide her imperfect past behavior while trying to become a more ethical person.

Who Is In The Good Place?

The The Good Place tenants consist of Eleanor Shellstrop, Chidi Anagonye, Tahani Al-Jamil, and Jason Mendoza. These are four people who we've selected to spend their afterlife in The Good Place because of their good behaviors that they contributed on earth. Protagonist Eleanor initially is mistakenly sent to the Good Place, so she hides this and decides to study ethics solely for self-preservation. She begins to gradually become more selfless and thoughtful as time goes by. Chidi's overwhelming knowledge of ethics often caused him to obsess over whether even his most insignificant actions were ethical. Tahani is exceptionally attractive and very fashionable; she was raised in high society England, and consequently was very well-educated. Jason was often considered dumb and was a misguided man who had major impulse issues.

What Is The Bad Place?

"The Bad Place" is essentially the equivalent of hell. It's where individuals go if their actions during their lives result in a net-negative moral score. The Bad Place is portrayed as a realm of eternal punishment and torment, but the show also uses it as a satirical critique of traditional concepts of the afterlife.
